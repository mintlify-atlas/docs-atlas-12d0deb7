## Roles at a glance

Every request is authenticated and authorized against one of six roles:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `PAYPAC` | Platform super-admin — full access |
| `ORGANIZER` | Event organizer — manages their own events, staff, and reports |
| `STAFF` | Event staff — validates tickets at the door |
| `STAFF_PROMOTER` | Staff with promoter capabilities |
| `PROMOTER` | External ticket promoter |
| `CUSTOMER` | End-user buyer |

<Tip>
  See [Roles & Permissions](/concepts/roles-permissions) for a complete breakdown of what each role can access.
</Tip>
